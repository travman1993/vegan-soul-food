Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Vegan Soul Food Kitchen (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (the “Site”).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and activity, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful.

Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of the Site, such as enabling navigation and access to certain features.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use the Site, which pages are visited most often, and how we can improve the experience.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies may remember your preferences, such as language, region, or other settings, to personalize your experience.
  • Advertising and marketing cookies: If we display third-party ads, these cookies may be used by ad networks to deliver relevant ads and track performance.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics tools or ad networks) that place their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies. Their use is governed by the privacy and cookie policies of those third parties.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember basic preferences where applicable.
  • Understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve layout and content.
  • Support the display and measurement of advertisements, if used.

How You Can Control Cookies

You have several options for controlling cookies:

  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies. Check your browser’s help section for specific instructions.
  • Opt-out tools: Some analytics and ad providers offer opt-out mechanisms for their tracking cookies.
  • Device settings: On mobile devices, you may be able to adjust settings to limit tracking or reset advertising identifiers.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the Site.

Cookie Consent Banner

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may see a cookie banner or pop-up on your first visit. This banner may ask for your consent to use certain types of cookies. Your choices may be stored in a cookie so we can remember your preferences.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
